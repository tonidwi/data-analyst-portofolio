# 📊 Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, regional sales distribution, and sales trends over time.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that can support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Questions

- Which product category contributes the highest sales?
- Which customer segment generates the highest revenue?
- Which regions have the strongest sales performance?
- How do sales change over time?
- Who are the top customers based on total sales?

---

## Dataset

- Source: Kaggle
- Dataset: Sample Superstore
- Records: 9800
- Features: 18

## Key Findings

### Sales Distribution

Most transactions are relatively small, while only a few transactions contribute very large sales values.

### Category Performance

Technology contributes the largest sales among all product categories.

### Regional Performance

West region records the highest sales contribution.

### Customer Segments

Consumer segment generates the largest portion of total revenue.

### Sales Trend

Monthly sales fluctuate throughout the observation period, indicating seasonal demand patterns.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing investment in high-performing product categories.
- Develop customer loyalty programs targeting top customers.
- Investigate regions with relatively low sales performance.
- Forecast inventory based on monthly sales trends.
- Monitor shipping performance to improve customer satisfaction.
